Interesting facts about chameleos

>
  • Chameleons are reptiles that are part of the iguana suborde
  • Changing skin colour is an important part of the communication among chameleons
  • Most chameleons have a prehensile tail that they use to wrap around tree branches

Interesting Facts about Pigeon

  1. Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals
  2. Pigeons are renowned for their outstanding navigational abilities
  3. Pigeons have excellent hearing abilities.

Interesting facts about Camels

>
  • Camels have three eyelids to protect their eyes from sand storms
  • Camels store water in their humps because of the scarcity of water in deserts
  • Camels have leathery lips to be able to eat the thorny desert plants
